Five-Stir

How to play:

Five words have been stirred together.
The first letter of each word is in the first column, the second letter in the second column, etc.
Your job is to pick out the five words, one at a time.
  • Correct words appear in Orange.
  • Incorrect words are split: the light gray letters are the beginning of a correct word.
    • Can you get the answer in just four guesses?
